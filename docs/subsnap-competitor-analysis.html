Money Saved ‚Äî Gamification
Competitive Advantages
01

Only App with AI Screenshot Scanning

Zero competitors offer this. Screenshot ‚Üí AI extracts name, price, billing cycle, renewal date, trial status. 2-second capture vs 30-second manual entry. The "wow moment" that drives conversion.

02

Cross-Platform (Flutter)

Bobby, Subscription Tracker, and Subby are all iOS-only. We're the only lightweight tracker on both platforms. 71% of global users are on Android ‚Äî that's a massive untapped market.

03

Trial-First Mentality

No competitor properly tracks free trials. We treat trials as urgent: countdown timers, escalating reminders, visual urgency. This alone saves users hundreds per year on forgotten trials.

04

Smart Escalating Reminders

Competitors offer basic "remind me X days before." We do 7-day heads up ‚Üí 3-day warning ‚Üí 1-day alert ‚Üí morning-of notification. Configurable per subscription, aggressive for trials.

05

Money Saved Gamification

Nobody tracks what you've saved by cancelling. We turn financial discipline into a game: running total, milestones ("You saved enough for a PS5!"), shareable achievements.

06

Privacy Without Compromise

Unlike Rocket Money ($14/mo + bank access), we need zero financial data. Screenshots are processed and discarded. All sub data stored locally/iCloud. Privacy IS the feature, not a limitation.

07

One-Time $4.99 ‚Äî No Irony

"No subscription needed to track your subscriptions." This is both a marketing hook and genuine differentiator. Competitors either charge subscriptions or have zero monetization (unsustainable).

08

3 Free AI Scans ‚Äî The Hook

Free users get 3 AI scans + 3 manual subs. They experience the magic, build the habit, hit the limit fast. The taster costs us $0.005/user but drives 4%+ conversion. Drug dealer economics.

Design Direction ‚Äî Our Aesthetic

Dark, Premium, Financial

Trackizer proved the market wants premium dark UI for finance apps. Bobby looks like a kids' app. Subby is clean but lacks gravitas. We go dark-first with subtle depth ‚Äî glass morphism hints, gradient accents, buttery animations. Think "Bloomberg meets Spotify."
